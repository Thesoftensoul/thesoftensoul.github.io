Meet Amanda

With over 20 years in healthcare and transformational wellness, I specialize in supporting high-achieving women who are tired of being the strong one—women who've spent their lives showing up for everyone else while silencing their own needs.

I understand the unique weight of navigating spaces that weren't designed for you, the exhaustion of proving your worth repeatedly, and the pain of carrying intergenerational wounds that were never yours to bear.

My approach combines evidence-based strategies with deep compassion. I hold space for your grief, your anger, your exhaustion, and your truth. Because healing isn't about becoming someone new—it's about reclaiming who you've always been beneath the armor.

You deserve more than survival. You deserve to thrive.
